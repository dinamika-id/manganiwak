1. Information We Collect

Mangan Iwak is designed with your privacy in mind. The app collects minimal data needed to provide its core functionality:

  • High Scores: When you save a score, only your chosen name and score are stored locally on your device. No personal identification is required.
  • Settings Preferences: Your audio, graphics quality, and other settings are saved locally on your device only.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic, anonymous device information (such as OS version and screen orientation) solely to improve app stability and performance. This data is never linked to your identity.

Mangan Iwak does not require account creation, login, or any form of personal identification. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used strictly to operate and improve the app:

  • Store and display your high scores within the app
  • Remember your settings (audio, graphics) between sessions
  • Diagnose and fix bugs to improve app stability
  • Understand basic usage patterns to guide game improvements

We never sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data collected by Mangan Iwak is stored locally on your device and is under your direct control. The app does not use any cloud servers or external databases to store your personal information.

You can clear all saved data at any time by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the app. High scores and settings are stored in the app's private storage area and are not accessible to other apps on your device.

We implement standard security practices to protect data stored on your device from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

Mangan Iwak may use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and basic crash reporting. Google Privacy Policy
  • Android System: The app runs on the Android OS, which has its own data handling per Google's Android privacy guidelines.

These services are used only as necessary for the app to function and are not used to track you or serve advertisements.

5. Children's Privacy

Mangan Iwak is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page
  • For significant changes, we will provide a notice within the app or update this page before the changes take effect

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
